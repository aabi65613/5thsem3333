Q1(i). In electrochemical machining (ECM) removal of metal from the work piece takes place

Answer (10th Std Level): ECM is like reverse electroplating! Workpiece = anode (+), tool = cathode (-). Current flows ‚Üí metal dissolves from workpiece into liquid ‚Üí tiny bits wash away. Like rusting super fast! So anodic dissolution ‚Üí a.

Q3. Explain working principle of EDM with neat sketch

Answer: EDM = Electric Discharge Machining. Spark jumps gap ‚Üí melts tiny metal bit ‚Üí dielectric flushes it.

  1. Tool (-) and job (+) close but no touch.
  2. High voltage ‚Üí spark ‚Üí 8000¬∞C heat.
  3. Metal melts ‚Üí vapor bubble ‚Üí explodes out.
  4. Oil washes debris.
